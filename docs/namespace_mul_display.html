MulDisplay 1.0.0.0

◆ ResponseMode

enum MulDisplay.ResponseMode

Only matters for the 2D mode. The minimum delay between 2 frames is determined by the DataRefreshDelay for both modes.

Enumerator
Synchronous 

The frame and its overlays will always be drawn, the BuildAndRefresh method will always wait for it to be drawn at least once before returning. The minimum delay between 2 frames is determined by the DataRefreshDelay for both modes.

Asynchronous 

The BuildAndRefresh method will return instantly, removing any sort of delay coming from the API, however certain frames may not be drawn if BuildAndRefresh is called too often. The minimum delay between 2 frames is determined by the DataRefreshDelay for both modes.
