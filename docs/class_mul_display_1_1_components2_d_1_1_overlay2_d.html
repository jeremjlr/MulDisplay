 The ZOffset of the overlay. Used to determine the overlay priority. Lower zOffset means the overlay will be closer to the camera and have priority over overlays with higher zOffset. The 2D camera uses an orthogonal projection matrix which makes it so distance from the camera doesn't affect the look of the overlays.
